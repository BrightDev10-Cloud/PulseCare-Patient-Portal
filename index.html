Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

Click on the "Book Appointment" button on the Dashboard or Appointments page, fill out the form with your preferred date, time, and doctor, then submit.

How can I request a prescription refill?

Navigate to the Prescriptions page, find your medication, and click the "Request Refill" button. Your doctor will be notified and process the request.

Can I access my medical records?

Yes! Go to the Medical Records page where you can view, download, and manage all your medical documents securely.
